Account and Data Deletion

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please follow the steps below:

  1. Email our support team at support@tolox.store with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".
  2. In the email, please include your account details (such as your email and passowrd) so we can identify your account.
  3. Our team will process your request and delete your account and data within 2 days. You will receive a confirmation email once this is done.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact our support team.
