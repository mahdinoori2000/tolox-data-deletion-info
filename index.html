Request Account Deletion

If you would like to delete your account, please follow the steps below:

  1. Send an email to our support team at support@tolox.store from the email address associated with your account.
  2. In your email, include a brief request to delete your account.
  3. Our team will process your request and delete your account and data within 2 days. You will receive a confirmation email once this is completed.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact our support team.
